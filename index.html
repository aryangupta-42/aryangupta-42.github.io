About Me
Hey there, I'm an undergraduate student at IIT Delhi. I'm fascinated by technology and space. Like, it's such a large universe, we can't possibly be alone, right?
Coding is my passion. I love to innovate and create new products. My field of work is primarily front-end web designing, but I'm well versed in back-end too and can create an elegant design from scratch. My hobbies include lawn tennis, swimming and listening to music. In my free time, I usually work on mini projects of my own or watch a movie or two. I feel that coding is my outlet into the creative world and can help me express myself to the world and even possibly create a mark upon it.
