During the winters of 2018, I represented IIT Delhi at TechFest, which is the technical festival of IIT Bombay.

Holonomic 4 Wheeled Drive

This is a Semi-autonomous bot created by me and a couple of other students in the robotics club at IIT Delhi.
The bot consists of 4 meccanum wheels to allow it to traverse any sort of rough terrain. These sort of wheels are also being used in the mars rover. It is a holonomic drive which means that it can move in any direction without changing it's orientation.

Furthermore, the bot is equiped with a powerful highly capable throwing mechanism which can pick up large low density curvi-linear objects of a certain height and throw it in the correct orientation with a range of over 2 meters.

Quad Leg Bot
Quad Leg Autonomous Robot
January
2019
June
2019
This quad leg robot was also designed by me, and my robocon team to take part in robocon 2019.
The brains of this bot was a raspberry pi 3 on which was responsible for executing the various python codes and sending the corresponding output signals to the servo motors.

The bot consisted of 4 legs, each of which worked on the principle of 5R-5Bar principle to allow motion of the foot to any point in the plane.
These legs joints are powered by torque sensing dynamixel motors for precise and accurate movement.
Furthemore the legs are made out of carbon-fiber to provide high strength while still keeping the weight very low.
